Rules & Regulations

Students are expected to maintain discipline and follow school guidelines at all times.

check_circle

Attend school regularly and be punctual.

check_circle

Leave applications are required for absences exceeding two days.

check_circle

Medical certificate is mandatory for absences of more than three days.

check_circle

Reason for every absence must be recorded in the school diary.

check_circle

Students cannot leave the campus during school hours without permission.

check_circle

Tests and examinations will not be rescheduled due to absence.

check_circle

Long leave for family functions or weddings is not permitted.

check_circle

No leave is granted during major school events and functions.

check_circle

Minimum 80% attendance is compulsory; below 60% may result in repeating the class.

check_circle

Minimum passing marks are 40% in every subject (Middle & High School).
