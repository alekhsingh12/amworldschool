Eligibility Criteria

Foundation Stage

Students must be 3+ years for Nursery and 4+ years for Kindergarten as of March 31st.

Primary School

Grade 1 applicants must have completed 5 years by the start of the academic session.

Senior Wing

Admissions for Grade 9 and 11 are subject to performance in the entrance exam and previous school track record.
